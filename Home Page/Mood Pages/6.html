The Forest of Discovery

A boy was bored with his repetitive life. He decided to walk into a nearby forest he'd never explored. He found a strange rock that looked like a sleeping giant, a stream that sang a gurgling tune, and a tree with a hollow in its trunk that looked like a secret doorway. By simply changing his scenery, he found a world of wonder he had never known was there. The world was full of stories; he just had to step outside his normal path to find them.
