The Whispering Stream

A student felt overwhelmed by her to-do list, which stretched on forever. She was so stressed she couldn't focus on anything. A wise teacher noticed her panic and led her to a stream. "Listen," he said. The student heard the constant, gentle flow of the water. "Does the stream worry about reaching the ocean? No, it just flows, one pebble at a time, one bend at a time." The student returned to her work and tackled one task at a time, no longer looking at the endless list but simply focusing on the current moment. The weight lifted, and she felt the calmness of the stream within her.
