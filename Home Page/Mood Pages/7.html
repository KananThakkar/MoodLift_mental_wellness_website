The Broken Compass

A lost traveler stood at a crossroads, his compass spinning wildly. Confused and frustrated, he sat down. A wise old woman appeared and said, "A good journey isn't about the destination, but about where your feet take you." The traveler put away his broken compass and simply chose a path. He found a beautiful waterfall and a village with warm, welcoming people. He realized the most beautiful things in life are often found when you stop trying to control the path.
