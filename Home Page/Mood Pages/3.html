The Worn-Out Map

A young man was lost, not in a forest, but in his life. He felt like he had no direction or purpose. He found a very old, tattered map that seemed to show no clear path. The map was covered in scribbles and notes from previous travelers. One note read: "The best paths are the ones you create." He decided to stop looking for a pre-made path and began to draw his own journey on the blank spaces of the map, filling it with his own dreams and goals. He realized that a lost person isn't a failure, but a pioneer.
