The Stitched Quilt

A woman felt hurt by unkind words someone had said to her. She felt like her heart was a torn piece of fabric. Her grandmother, a quilter, saw her sadness. She gave her a needle and thread and showed her how to stitch the fabric back together. The grandmother explained, "Every stitch is an act of forgiveness, for yourself and others." As the woman mended the fabric, her heart began to heal, and the wound became a beautiful, unique part of her story.
