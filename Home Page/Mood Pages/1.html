The Unseen Gardener

A woman was overcome with sadness, feeling like her life was a barren garden. One day, a friend gave her a single seed in a tiny pot. The woman was too heartbroken to care for it, but she put it on her windowsill anyway. Every morning, a single drop of dew would fall on it, and every evening, a stray sunbeam would warm it. The woman didn't notice this until a small sprout pushed through the soil. She realized that even when she felt incapable of tending to her own soul, the universe was still quietly nurturing it. Her sadness didn't disappear, but it was joined by a gentle sense of hope.
