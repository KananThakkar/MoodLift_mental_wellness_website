The Weight of Stones

A man carried a heavy bag of stones, each one representing a past mistake. He was consumed by guilt. He met a kind stranger who asked him to empty the bag. The man was hesitant but finally did. The stranger picked up one stone and said, "You can learn from this, but you don't have to carry it." As he placed each stone on the ground, the man felt the weight lift from his shoulders. He left the stones behind and walked away, finally free.
