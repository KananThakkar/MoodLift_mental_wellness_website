Yoga for Stress and Anxiety

Introduction

Yoga is more than a physical practice — it’s a way to calm the nervous system and quiet the mind. With its focus on mindful breathing and gentle movement, yoga offers an effective way to ease stress and anxiety.

Benefits of Yoga

Yoga lowers cortisol levels, reduces heart rate, and promotes relaxation. Studies show that consistent yoga practice can improve sleep, boost mood, and create a sense of inner peace.

Best Poses for Stress Relief

Some beginner-friendly poses that are especially soothing include:

  • Child’s Pose (Balasana): Releases tension in the back and mind.
  • Legs Up the Wall (Viparita Karani): Improves circulation and calms the nervous system.
  • Cat-Cow Stretch: Eases back tension and connects breath with movement.

Conclusion

Practicing yoga for even 10–15 minutes a day can bring balance and calm into your life. It’s a gentle reminder that slowing down and breathing deeply are powerful steps toward better mental wellness.
