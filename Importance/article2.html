5 Daily Habits to Reduce Stress

Introduction

Stress is a normal part of life, but too much of it can be harmful. By building small healthy habits into your routine, you can lower stress and boost happiness.

Daily Habits

1. Start your day with deep breathing.
2. Spend 15 minutes in nature.
3. Keep a gratitude journal.
4. Move your body — stretch, walk, or do yoga.
5. Disconnect from screens before bedtime.

These small habits, practiced consistently, help you feel calmer and more in control of your day-to-day life.
